BCIS 1110 Generative AI Policy

Artificial Intelligence (ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini) is utilized as a tool for assistance—not substitution. Students are expected to understand all submitted work and provide formal prompt citations.

APA Prompt Citation Format: "Ask your AI tool at the end of a session: 'Please cite this interaction in APA format and include the original prompt.'"
